About Me

I am an experienced human genomics scientist with in-depth knowledge of DNA sequencing data analysis who is especially interested in delivering high-quality results through reproducible, scalable bioinformatic pipeline development. I believe that borrowing from the field of software engineering is a necessity for bioinformaticians, and I require that my development teams implement best practices regarding version control, code review, and unit and black box testing. I am passionate about reproducibility: I use workflow management systems like Snakemake with conda and Singularity/Docker containers to manage workflows and environments, and Jupyter notebooks to record and communicate my findings.
